How to Download and Set Up Ledger Live

Ready to begin? The Ledger Live download process is straightforward. Just follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official website: ledger.com/start
  2. Click on the "Download Ledger Live" option visible on the page.
  3. Choose the version that matches your system—Windows, Mac, or Linux.
  4. Download will begin automatically based on your selection.
  5. Once installed, connect your Ledger hardware wallet to your computer or mobile using the provided cable.
  6. Open the app and follow the guided setup instructions.
  7. If this is your first time, select the “Initialize as new device” option.
  8. Create a secure PIN code on the device screen.
  9. Write down your recovery phrase and store it somewhere safe and offline.
  10. Confirm the recovery phrase to finalize the setup.

Once done, you’re ready to use Ledger Live to manage and track all your activity. Whether you're on desktop or mobile, the process stays consistent.

How to Access Your Wallet Again – Recovery Steps

If you've lost access to your app or had to reset your device, recovering your data is simple, provided you have the secret phrase saved during the initial setup.

Here’s how you can restore access:

  1. Open the ledger live app.
  2. Choose the "Restore device using recovery phrase" option.
  3. Enter your recovery phrase using the Ledger device.
  4. Set a new PIN for access.
  5. Once confirmed, your wallet will be restored, along with access to all previous accounts.

Just ensure your recovery phrase is written down somewhere safe and not stored online. This is the key to your security and should be handled with the utmost caution.
